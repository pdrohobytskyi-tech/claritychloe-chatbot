Everything you need to navigate your ADHD with compassion. Click on any tool to see how to use it best.

Notion Planner

Digital • Science-backed

🚀 Quick Start (5 minutes):

  1. Open "Today" - Write max 3 key actions (not 10!)
  2. Choose Energy Level - Low? Use only ⚡Low tasks
  3. Activate Overwhelm Mode - See only 1 task at a time

💡 Pro Tip: Don't know where to start? Go to Routines and activate "Morning Anchor" - 3 fixed steps to start.

90-Day Journey

Workbook • Emotional

📍 How to use it (ADHD-friendly):

✅ You can:

  • Start on any day (no order needed)
  • Skip days when you can't
  • Go back and re-read
  • Write 2 lines or 2 pages - both are fine

🎯 If you're stuck: Go to Day 7 (Acceptance Day) - it's the perfect emotional reset when you feel you're "not doing enough".

It's not a perfect journal. It's a gentle mirror.

ADHD Reset Pack

Emergency Kit • 13 Pages

🆘 Which page to use when:

EMERGENCY (Acute crisis):

  • • Pg. 3: Brain Dump → Empty everything onto paper
  • • Pg. 5: Priority Bridge → Pick just 1 thing to do NOW
  • • Pg. 7: 5-Min Sensory Reset → Calm the nervous system

🔄 RESET MODE (Restart):

  • • Morning Anchor (p.8) → 3 fixed steps to start
  • • Midday Reset (p.9) → When you lose the thread after lunch
  • • Weekly Reset (p.12) → Sunday evening prep

💡 Pro Tip: Keep the Reset Pack always visible (not in a drawer). It only works if it's "grab & go" in moments of panic.

Cleaning Bundle

Home Hacks • System

⚡ The 3 most effective hacks:

1. 5-Minute Blitz → Timer + 1 zone. Stop when it rings. Done = dopamine.

2. One-Touch Rule → Pick something up? Put it back immediately. Stops accumulation.

3. Zone Cleaning → Monday = kitchen. Tuesday = bathroom. Not everything at once.

When you feel "I can't do it": Open the Room Checklist and do just 1 task. Even "emptying the trash" counts.

PDF Planner

Designed for Mental Overload → Specific calming colors chosen to not overstimulate you when you're overwhelmed.

It's not mandatory to fill every space. Use only what you need.
